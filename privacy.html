Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Data We Collect

To provide a reliable life-saving service, we collect limited information from our donors:

  • Full Name & Blood Group
  • Contact Number
  • Current Address (Village/Town)
  • Birth Date & Gender

Donor Security

Female Donor Protection: We take security seriously. Contact numbers of female donors are never displayed publicly. They can only be reached through our authorized Admin.

Male donor contact numbers are visible to searchers to ensure the fastest possible response during emergencies.

How We Use Data

Your information is used strictly for:

  • Connecting blood seekers with donors.
  • Updating you on blood-related emergencies.
  • Ensuring the accuracy of our donor database.

We never sell or share your data with third-party advertisers.

Your Control

You have full control over your profile. Using the Update tab on our homepage, you can change your phone number or set your status to "Not Ready" if you are unable to donate temporarily.

By using this website, you agree to our terms of service.
