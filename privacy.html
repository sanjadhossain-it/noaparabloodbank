Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2025

Information We Collect

When you register as a donor at Noapara Blood Bank, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name & Age: To identify you as a valid donor.
  • Blood Group: To match you with patients in need.
  • Phone Number: To allow emergency seekers to contact you.
  • Location: To find donors near Raozan and Noapara area.
Why we collect it: This data is collected solely for humanitarian purposes—to facilitate emergency blood transfusions and save lives.

Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to serve ads on our website. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your visit to this and other websites on the Internet.

  • DART Cookies: Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users.
  • User Choice: You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network privacy policy.

We also use standard log files that track IP addresses, browser types, and timestamps to analyze trends and improve user experience.

How We Protect Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent donor personal identification information to others. For the safety of our female donors, their contact numbers are hidden from public view and are only shared via our verified Admin helpline.

Your Consent

By using our website and registering your information, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. You have the right to update or remove your data at any time via the "Update" tab on our homepage.
