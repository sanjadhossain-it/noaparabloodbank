Frequently Asked Questions

How to report a fire emergency?

Immediately dial 999 or call the Raozan Fire Station directly at 01730336655.

Does Fire Service provide Ambulance?

Yes, the Fire Service & Civil Defense provides emergency ambulance services for accidents and fire victims.
