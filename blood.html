General Questions

Is it safe to donate blood?
Yes, blood donation is a safe clinical procedure. All equipment is sterile and used only once for your safety.
How often can I donate?
Generally, you can donate whole blood every 56 days (8 weeks) to allow your body to replenish iron levels.
